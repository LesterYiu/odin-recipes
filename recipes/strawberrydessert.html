A trendy 3-step strawberry dessert that will leave your husband speechless

A multi layered puff pastry with crème fraîche, and strawberries.

This trendy Tiktok 4-ingredient strawberry dessert will make dessert time a lot more fun.

Ingredients

  • 2 sheets of puff pastry
  • 1 cup creme fraiche
  • 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
  • 4 ounces strawberries

Steps

  1. Cut 2 sheets of puff pastry in half. Bake 3 of them at 400°F Just 4 ingredients! for 18 minutes.
  2. In a medium bowl, whip 1 cup crème fraîche and 2 Tbsp. confectioners' sugar until fluffy.
  3. Layer pastry, cream and 4 oz. strawberries; repeat. Dust with confectioners' sugar. Serves
